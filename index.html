Who We Are

Experts in External Wall Fire Safety Since 2020

EWS1 was founded in the immediate aftermath of the EWS1 form's introduction in December 2019.

Our founding team of RICS-qualified fire engineers recognised a sector in crisis and built EWS1 to be the most rigorous assessment service in the country.

Today we have completed over 2,400 assessments across England, Scotland and Wales.
